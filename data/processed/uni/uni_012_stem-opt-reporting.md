## On this Page

Now that your [STEM OPT Extension](https://isso.columbia.edu/content/f-1-stem-opt-extension) has been approved, keep the following things in mind:

### Submit a STEM OPT validation report in Compass every 6 months

The 6-, 12-, 18-, and 24-month STEM OPT validation reports are based on the start date of your STEM OPT Extension's EAD card.

The earliest we can accept the 6-, 12-, 18-, and 24-month STEM OPT validation reports is 30 days before they are due.

The latest we can update your F-1 SEVIS record and SEVP Portal information is up to 30 days after they are due.

### Report employment changes within 10 days

In addition to STEM OPT validation reports, you need to report any change to your name, address, place of employment, or end of employment within 10 days.

### You are limited to a total of 150 days without employment

You are limited to a total of 150 days without employment during the period encompassing both Post-Completion OPT and the STEM OPT extension.

### You and your employer have STEM OPT reporting obligations

All STEM OPT employers must be enrolled in [E-Verify](https://www.e-verify.gov/).

A complete and signed [I-983 STEM OPT Training Plan](https://www.ice.gov/doclib/sevis/pdf/i983.pdf) is required for all STEM OPT employment.


## Reporting Requirements During STEM OPT

### 1Submit your 6-month validation report

6 months *after *your STEM OPT EAD card’s start date, use Compass to report your current U.S. address and confirm your employer information.

You will also be asked to provide a copy of your STEM OPT EAD card.

### 2Submit your 12-month validation report

12 months *after *your STEM OPT EAD card’s start date, use Compass to report your current U.S. address.

You and your employer will also need to complete your Evaluation of Student Progress.

The Evaluation of Student Progress can be found on the** top half of page 5** of the

[I-983 STEM OPT Training Plan](https://www.ice.gov/doclib/sevis/pdf/i983.pdf).


### 3Submit your 18-month Validation report

18 months *after *your STEM OPT EAD card’s start date, use Compass to report your current U.S. address and confirm your employer information.

### 4Submit your 24-month Validation Report

24 months *after *your STEM OPT EAD card’s start date, use Compass to report the end of your OPT STEM Extension authorization period.

You and your employer will need to complete your Final Evaluation.

The Final Evaluation can be found on the** bottom half of page 5** of the

[I-983 STEM OPT Training Plan](https://www.ice.gov/doclib/sevis/pdf/i983.pdf).


### 5Report New Employer(s)

Use this report if you are ending employment with a previous employer ** and **have a new employer.

You will be asked for the end date of your previous employment and the start date of your new employment.

For your previous employer, you and your previous employer will need to complete your Final Evaluation. The Final Evaluation can be found on the** bottom half of page 5** of the

[I-983 STEM OPT Training Plan](https://www.ice.gov/doclib/sevis/pdf/i983.pdf).

For your new employer, you and your new employer will need to complete a new Training Plan. This will require ** pages 1-4 **of

**the**

[I-983 STEM OPT Training Plan](https://www.ice.gov/doclib/sevis/pdf/i983.pdf).

If you are only adding a new employer, you will Report your Additional Employer.

If you are only ending employment, you will Report your Employment Ended.

### 6Report your Additional Employer

Use this report if you have an additional employer.

You and your additional employer will need to complete a new Training Plan. This will require ** pages 1-4 **of

**the**

[I-983 STEM OPT Training Plan](https://www.ice.gov/doclib/sevis/pdf/i983.pdf).


### 7Report your Employment Ended

Use this report if you are ending employment with an employer.

You and your employer will need to complete your Final Evaluation. The Final Evaluation can be found on the** bottom half of page 5** of the

[I-983 STEM OPT Training Plan](https://www.ice.gov/doclib/sevis/pdf/i983.pdf).


### 8Report your Departure from the US and that you will not use/resume STEM OPT

Use this report to notify the ISSO that you have departed the U.S. and will not use or resume STEM OPT. The ISSO will permanently complete your F-1 SEVIS record.

Your Columbia I-20 can no longer be used to re-enter the US. Your EAD card will no longer be valid for employment. Your current F-1 SEVIS record will no longer be valid to [transfer to another school](https://isso.columbia.edu/content/transferring-your-f-1-sevis-record-another-school) or [begin a new program](https://isso.columbia.edu/content/extending-begin-new-program-f-j-students). You may obtain a new I-20 via a new SEVIS record should you gain admission to an F-1 eligible program in the future. If applicable, any F-2 dependent record(s) will also be completed.* *

Only submit this report after you have departed the U.S.

You and your employer will need to complete your Final Evaluation. The Final Evaluation can be found on the** bottom half of page 5** of the

[I-983 STEM OPT Training Plan](https://www.ice.gov/doclib/sevis/pdf/i983.pdf).


### 9Report your New Immigration Status

Use this report to notify the ISSO about your new approved status. The ISSO will permanently complete your F-1 SEVIS record.

You and your employer will need to complete your Final Evaluation. The Final Evaluation can be found on the** bottom half of page 5** of the

[I-983 STEM OPT Training Plan](https://www.ice.gov/doclib/sevis/pdf/i983.pdf).

You will also be asked for proof of your new immigration status (e.g. [I-94 Arrival Record](https://i94.cbp.dhs.gov/home) indicating your new status; I-797 Change of Status Approval Notice; Permanent Resident Card).


### 10Report your Material Changes to I-983

Use this report if there are any changes to your learning objectives or your employer’s commitments as documented on your [I-983 STEM OPT Training Plan](https://www.ice.gov/doclib/sevis/pdf/i983.pdf).

This can also include changes related to your Employer Identification Number (EIN), compensation, hours worked per week, or your employer's name or address.

## How to Report During STEM OPT

Log in to Compass to submit your updates and changes while on STEM OPT.

## After You Report

### 1An updated I-20 after reporting is not required

The ISSO does not automatically provide an I-20 after your STEM OPT is approved or after employment reports are submitted. Only if needed, you may request an updated I-20 that shows your approval and employer information by following the instructions on our [I-20 Reprints](https://isso.columbia.edu/content/travel-signatures-and-i-20ds-2019-reprints-f-and-j-students#) page.

### 2Updating your US address only

If a change of your US address is the only update you need to report, go to the[ Change of Address](https://isso.columbia.edu/content/changing-your-address) page.

### 3Travel and STEM OPT

Review the [documents](https://isso.columbia.edu/content/travel-during-f-1-opt) required to re-enter the US as an F-1 student on STEM OPT.

Check page 2 of your current I-20 and ensure the travel signature is dated within 6 months of your planned re-entry. If you require an updated I-20, you can request one by following the instructions on our [I-20 Reprints](https://isso.columbia.edu/content/travel-signatures-and-i-20ds-2019-reprints-f-and-j-students#) page.

### 4Submitting a validation report while unemployed

If you are unemployed during STEM OPT and your validation report is due, then submit your report based on the information of your last employer.

## The SEVP Portal and STEM OPT

You cannot submit your STEM OPT employment changes and validation reports using the SEVP Portal. You must submit them to the ISSO using Compass. The ISSO will then update your SEVIS record, which will be reflected in your SEVP Portal.

You will receive notifications from SEVP regarding your 6-, 12-, 18-, and 24-month STEM OPT validation report requirements. The ISSO can update your SEVIS record up to 30 days after they are due. It is your responsibility to submit your reports in a timely manner.

If you have any questions regarding SEVP emails related to your STEM OPT, you may contact SEVP directly via their contact information [here](https://studyinthestates.dhs.gov/sevp-portal-help).
