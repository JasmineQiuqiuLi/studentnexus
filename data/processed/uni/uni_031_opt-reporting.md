# OPT Reporting

Information about OPT/STEM OPT employment reporting.


**All F1 Students with approved OPT/STEM OPT must report their employment as required by federal regulations. **

- Students with
authorization are required to report their employment now using the**12-month post-completion OPT**.[myGloba](http://myglobal.njit.edu)l International Student Portal

**While you are still welcome to continue reporting your employment and any employment changes directly through the** [ SEVP Portal](https://www.njit.edu/global/SEVP-portal),

**the OPT Reporting E-Form will need to be submitted to OGI regardless.**

- Students with
authorization are required to report their employment using**24-month STEM OPT Extension**.[myGlobal](https://myglobal.njit.edu)International Student Portal*STEM Extension students may not use the SEVP Portal to report changes in their STEM employment.*


must continue to report changes of address, phone numbers, and email addresses in the**All students**[Highlander Pipeline](https://portal.njit.edu/)so the university has up to date contact information for you; however, you may report this information directly in your SEVP portal as well.

**Employment changes/reporting may be submitted at any time prior to the change occurring or reporting due date, and must be submitted no greater than 10 days after the change occurring/reporting due date.**

**Students without an active SEVIS record cannot report the end of STEM OPT employment.**

- This applies to students with approved change of status (eg: H1B) or who have traveled and re-entered the US with a different visa.
- Therefore, students who do not report the end of employment prior to their SEVIS inactivation may send page 5 of the I-983 to global, but they will not be able to report the end of employment in myGlobal or in SEVIS.
